Rules & Regulations

Rule 1.1: No Cheating or Third-Party Software
Using any illegal hacks, modifications, game-engine exploits, aimbots, wallhacks, macros, or external scripts is strictly forbidden. Automatic and manual monitoring is active.

Rule 1.2: Account Sharing & Boosting
Players must play on their registered ArenaX account and game UID only. Playing on another player's behalf or account sharing will result in tournament bans.

Rule 1.3: Collusion & Match-Fixing
Collusion, teaming up in solo modes, trading kills, or intentionally throwing matches is highly illegal and leads to immediate prize forfeiture and ban.

Rule 2.1: Fake Deposit Submissions
Entering fake transaction IDs (TXN) or uploading mock payment receipts is considered fraud and will trigger an immediate permanent account ban.

Rule 2.2: Withdrawal Timelines
All withdrawals are processed securely within 24 to 48 hours. Flooding support tickets with status checks slows queue processing.

Rule 2.3: Entry Fee & Prize Disputes
Entry fees are non-refundable once the tournament starts. Prizes are distributed according to verified server logs and official admin decisions.

Rule 3.1: Toxicity & Harassment
Toxicity, toxic comments in chat rooms, hate speech, spamming, or abuse directed toward players or ArenaX staff members will lead to temporary/permanent mute.

Rule 3.2: Staff & Player Impersonation
Impersonating ArenaX administrators, game moderators, or other high-profile players to gain benefits is strictly banned.

Rule 4.1: Single Account Limit
Each player is permitted only one ArenaX account. Creating multiple accounts to farm referrals, coupons, or exploit sign-up benefits will result in all related accounts being blocked.

Rule 4.2: Emulator & Hardware Bypass
All tournaments are mobile-only unless specified. Emulating phone environments or using external mouse/keyboard adapters to play in mobile brackets is illegal.

Terms & Conditions

Welcome to ArenaX!

By registering for or playing in ArenaX tournaments, you agree to comply fully with these Terms and Conditions.

1. Fair Play & Anti-Cheat

Cheating, exploiting game bugs, using third-party macro software/scripts, or collusion with other players is strictly forbidden. Admins monitor matches and can ban accounts and forfeit entry fees without any appeal.

2. Wallet, Deposits & Withdrawals

All deposits are reviewed by administration. Entering fake transaction IDs (TXN) will trigger an immediate permanent account ban. Withdrawals settle within 24-48 hours. Coins inside ArenaX cannot be transferred directly to other user accounts.

3. Content Restrictions

Harassment, hate speech, spamming, and toxic behavior in public chats or support rooms is prohibited and will result in temporary or permanent messaging restrictions.

4. Account Loss

Guest account data is stored locally in your browser. Clearing your cache or browser cookies will lead to loss of access, and guest accounts cannot be recovered.

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to ArenaX

We are committed to securing your personal information and ensuring full transparency.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your email address, display name, profile avatar, and system metadata during registration/sign-in. Your gameplay logs, transaction histories, and messaging records are stored in a secure cloud database (Firestore).

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used to maintain your profile, track balances, match you in tournaments, provide support, and prevent fraudulent actions or cheating.

3. Third Party Policy

ArenaX does not sell, lease, or distribute your email address or personal statistics to any third-party marketing companies.

4. Your Control

You can modify your display profile settings, turn off certain notifications in settings, or request full account deletion via support.

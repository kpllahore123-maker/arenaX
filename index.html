General Rules
  • Register before tournament deadline — no exceptions.
  • Be online 10 minutes before your match starts.
  • No-shows = automatic disqualification.
  • Cheating or hacking leads to permanent ban.
  • Match results are final once confirmed by both parties.
Team Rules
  • Teams must have 2–5 players.
  • One player, one team per tournament.
  • Team names must be appropriate.
  • Roster changes allowed up to 24 hours before event.
Prizes & Wallet
  • Prizes distributed within 72 hours of tournament end.
  • Guest accounts have restricted prize withdrawals.
  • All prizes awarded in AX Coins via wallet.
  • Prize disputes must be raised within 48 hours.
Prohibited Conduct

  • Exploiting bugs intentionally is forbidden.
  • Account sharing leads to disqualification.
  • Collusion or match-fixing = permanent ban.
Anti-Cheat
  • Automated and manual anti-cheat on all matches.
  • Suspicious activity triggers mandatory review.
  • Confirmed cheating = permanent ban, no appeal.
  • Report via support chat with screenshots.

Information & Rules

General Rules
  • All players must register before the tournament deadline.
  • Players must be online 10 minutes before match start.
  • No-shows result in automatic disqualification.
  • Cheating or hacking leads to permanent ban.
  • Match results are final once confirmed by both parties.
Team Composition
  • Teams must have 2–5 players.
  • Each player can only be on one team per tournament.
  • Team name must not contain offensive language.
  • Roster changes allowed up to 24 hours before event.
Prizes & Rewards
  • Prizes distributed within 72 hours of tournament end.
  • Guest accounts may have restricted prize withdrawals.
  • All prizes awarded in ArenaX Coins via Wallet.
  • Prize disputes must be raised within 48 hours.
Prohibited Behaviour
  • Harassment, hate speech, or abuse not tolerated.
  • Exploiting game bugs intentionally is forbidden.
  • Account sharing not allowed.
  • VPN usage may be restricted per game rules.
Anti-Cheat
  • ArenaX uses automated and manual anti-cheat monitoring.
  • Suspicious activity triggers a review period.
  • Players found guilty face permanent bans.
  • Report suspicious players via support chat.
